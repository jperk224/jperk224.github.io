Hi, I'm Jeff.

I've been working in FinTech for more than eight years in SaaS eBanking and ePayment spaces. During that time I've developed a passion for finance, tech, and delivering great software. I've learned alot about tech and UX during this time. I have a passion for learning new concepts and technologies and strive for nothing less than delivering the best products to market.

I currently serve as a Product Owner for an ePayment vendor, leading a team responsible for the development and support of our payment processing platform. This platform comprises web service integration with various vendors and applications, as well as a robust web application and hardware integration for over-the-counter payments. I recently led transformation of this platform into an SOA solution using RESTful APIs. I've gained valuable insights into the characteristics and methodologies that define great product leadership and unmatched product development. Humility must be at the heart of any great product leader. I strive to foster a collaborative approach to software development and delivery. I rely heavily on valuable insights from engineering and operations, as well as client feedback and stakeholder direction. I create a team dynamic where individuals are the owners of their delverables, challenging them to advocate their case when strategic and development decisions must be made. I evangelize iterative and incremental development practices, ensuring proofs of concept are not over-engineered and velocity and adaptability are maintained. Thanks for visiting my site!
